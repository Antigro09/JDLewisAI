# ContractorAI — Promo Video (30s, 16:9)

A 30-second horizontal brand ad for ContractorAI, produced with Higgsfield AI in the
app's visual language (brand orange `#f97316`/`#ea580c`, warm off-white `#fff7ed`,
deep charcoal, rounded cards, clean geometric sans) — styled like a modern
Apple / Claude / Google product ad. Product-focused: no actors, no usage footage.

**Final video — v2 fast-paced cut (1280×720, 30s, continuous voiceover + score):**
<https://d8j0ntlcm91z4.cloudfront.net/user_3G6RqDndVG2Mizpxv0qQWhGyAIN/hf_20260706_054228_ba287abd-17cb-4b55-ada8-6a2f7a95c040.mp4>

v2 replaces the six independently-animated blocks of v1 with two continuous 15-second
Seedance 2.0 multi-shot takes (three fast-cut shots each, directed by the v1 keyframes
as references) so the cuts are seamless, the score is continuous, and the voiceover
runs wall-to-wall with no frozen frames or dead air.

## Production notes (v2)

- **Keyframes:** 6 stills generated with Nano Banana Pro (16:9, 1K), two of them
  conditioned on the real app icon (`public/icons/icon-512.png`) for brand fidelity.
- **Motion:** two continuous 15s Seedance 2.0 multi-shot generations (fast mode, 720p,
  native audio), each directed by three keyframes as image references — fast hard cuts,
  kinetic camera, continuous electronic score per half.
- **Voiceover:** Higgsfield Seed Audio, preset voice "Sterling"; two continuous takes
  duration-calibrated to ~14s so each fills its 15s window without frozen frames or
  delayed starts.
- **Assembly:** Higgsfield explainer-video assembler, two 15s blocks — exact 30.0s.
- **QA:** verified scene-by-scene with Higgsfield video analysis (copy, pacing,
  transitions, VO alignment, no freezes).
- **Cost:** ≈190 credits total on the Starter plan (v1 + v2).
